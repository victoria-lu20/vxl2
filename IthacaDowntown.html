Ithaca Downtown

Filled with boutiques, art galleries, and restaurants, the Ithaca Commons is a popular destination for most college students. Overwhelmed by the number of choices in the area? Feel free to check out what's offered below!

Price Range: $

Waffle Frolic

As a local restaurant founded by two Ithaca college students, Waffle Frolic is a bustling destination for both college students and tourists alike. With both sweet and savory options, this place is definitely the place to hit up when you're craving waffles or a quick fix.

Price Range: $$

Simeon's American Bistro

This restaurant has some of the best brunch options, and their eggs benedict is sooo worth it. Although this is a middle-tiered restaurant, they have great food at reasonable prices.

The Rook

Comfort food in a modern setting with exposed brick walls? Yes, please! Plus, The Rook offers some of the best donuts as their desserts too!

Saigon Kitchen

One of the most Yelped restaurants of Ithaca, Saigon Kitchen offers authentic Vietnamese cuisine. Be sure to get there early if you have a large party because they don't take reservations and lines do get pretty long!

Price Range: $$$

Gola Osteria

As the go-to place for occasions, Gola Osteria offers authentic Italian in a classic ambience. Tucked away in the Quarry Arms building, this place is truly a hole in the wall - a gem waiting to be discovered.
