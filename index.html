Ithaca Gorges

Hungry? Don't know where to go? Hit up the Commons and Collegetown!

Ithaca, home to both Cornell and Ithaca College, has a reputation for its ~gorge-ous~ views and ~above-average~ dining halls, but what about the restaurants in and around the colleges?

Whether in Collegetown or in Downtown Ithaca, you are bound to find restaurants that satisfy all kinds of cravings. Welcome to Eats Around Ithaca, where you will find rankings of the various restaurants around Ithaca, based on price range and taste.
