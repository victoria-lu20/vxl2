Ithaca Mall

In the opposite direction of the Commons, Ithaca Mall offers a variety of stores, including Target, DSW, Ulta's Beauty, Best Buy, and Dick's Sporting Goods. But what food options do they offer and what's good? Read on to find out!

Price Range: $

Zocalo Mexican Bar & Grill

Tucked in Ithca Mall, this restaurant is an ideal place to get your Mexican mix while comfortably sitting in the midst of the mall, where you can continue your shopping after delving into a hearty meal.

Easy Wok

Conveniently located in the food court of Ithaca Mall, Easy Wok offers Chinese take-out at reasonable prices. Want a break from the shopping? Hit up the Ithaca Mall food court!

Price Range: $$

Ciao Wood-Fire Pizza & Pasta

In a rustic ambience, this restaurant offers traditional wood-fire pizza. Recommended by locals, this place is situated right by the YMCA and is close to the mall, which is a plus for those who worry about transportation from the mall to the restaurant.

Ithaca Sumo

With elaborate decor and skilled chefs, Sumo offers quality hibachi and sushi. Located in a strip mall right by Ithaca Mall, Sumo is conveniently located for college students. Make sure to hit up Sumo if you're with family or with friends!
