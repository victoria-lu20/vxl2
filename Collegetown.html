Collegetown

Right past the Law School and the Schwartz Performing Arts Center, Collegetown is the hub for college students. It offers a variety of options to satisfy anything - from late night cravings to a proper meal!

Price Range: $

Collegetown Bagels

Known belovingly as CTB by college students, this place is a popular destination. With locations in both Commons and in Collegetown, be sure to hit up CTB for a quick bagel fix to see where the rave comes from.

Wings Over Ithaca

As the go-to place for late-night cravings, Wings is the most crowded at 1-2am on weekends. This is for sure one of the essential places to go and try, if you're looking for a true college experience in Ithaca.

So Poong

Offering authentic Korean food at inexpensive prices, So Poong is the go-to place for comfort Asian cuisine. With fast service, you can enjoy your food on the go or in the comfort of the restaurant.

Price Range: $$

Rulloff's Ithaca

Situated right next to CTB, Rulloff's offers pub plates and cocktails. With comfort American food, Rulloff's is the place to go when you're craving a good burger or good ole fish and chips. They also offer weekly events, such as Bingo/Trivia night!

Koko's

A popular destination amongst Cornell students, Koko's offers traditional Korean dishes in a casual ambience. Whether you're craving Korean bibimbap or seafood pancakes, Koko's is the place to hit up and try at least once!

Mehak

The smell of curry and freshly baked bread greets you as you walk into the door of Mehak. Known for their buffets, Mehak offers a great environment for group meals.
